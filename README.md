## Overview

**My Time Matter** is an iOS app for salary-exempt employees to track work activity and see their **true hourly equivalent**. Many professionals work beyond the standard 40-hour workweek, and this app helps them understand what their salary is really paying them for their time.

The app tracks activities such as:

* Office work
* Emails
* Phone calls
* After-hours tasks

It calculates your effective hourly rate and provides insights into how additional hours impact your compensation.

---

## Features

### Free Tier

* Manual time tracking
* Basic calculation of hourly rate
* Weekly summaries

### Pro Tier (In-App Purchase)

* Historical tracking (monthly, yearly)
* Advanced work categories (after-hours, weekends, remote work)
* Alerts for hours or rate thresholds
* Export reports (PDF/CSV)
* Scenario modeling for raises or reduced hours

## Usage

1. Open the app
2. Add a new task or activity

5. Upgrade to Pro to access advanced analytics and export options
